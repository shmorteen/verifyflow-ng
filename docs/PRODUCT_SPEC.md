# Product Spec: VerifyFlow NG

VerifyFlow NG helps Nigerian businesses collect identity information, documents, consent, and verification evidence through a secure digital onboarding workflow.

## Problem

Businesses onboard customers, tenants, staff, students, cooperative members, agents, and vendors using WhatsApp, paper forms, scattered ID photos, and manual spreadsheets.

## Solution

A simple onboarding platform where a business sends a link to an applicant. The applicant submits identity details, uploads documents/selfie, agrees to consent, and the business reviews the record from a dashboard.

## Target Verticals

1. Real estate and tenant verification
2. Cooperatives and loan businesses
3. HR and recruitment
4. Schools and training centers
5. SMEs managing agents/vendors

## Positioning

> Identity onboarding and compliance automation for Nigerian businesses.
