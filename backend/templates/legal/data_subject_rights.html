Requests that may be available

Depending on applicable law and the organization responsible for the onboarding record, an individual may request access, correction, deletion, restriction, portability, or information about how personal data is used.

How requests will work

A production request channel will be published after legal review. VerifyFlow NG or the responsible organization may need limited information to locate a record and safely confirm the requester's identity before acting.

Protect your identity information

Do not submit a full NIN, BVN, identity document, selfie, password, or API credential through an unconfirmed email address or general contact channel. Verification instructions will be provided through the approved request process.

Complaints and appeals

The final process will explain response timelines, lawful exceptions, appeal options, and how to contact the appropriate data protection authority.
