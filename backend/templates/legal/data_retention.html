Data Retention Policy

Keeping records only as long as needed

Placeholder content - legal review required. Retention periods below are not yet defined and must not be used as a production retention schedule.

Policy principles

  • Keep applicant information only for a documented business or legal purpose.
  • Limit access while records are retained.
  • Delete or anonymize records when the approved period expires.
  • Pause deletion where a valid legal hold or dispute requires preservation.

Records requiring approved schedules

Applicant profiles, consent records, identity documents, selfies, verification summaries, reports, audit logs, account records, and backups each require a defined retention period, owner, deletion method, and legal basis.

Deletion and backups

Production procedures must cover deletion from primary storage, provider systems, exports, and backups, including the time required for encrypted backup copies to expire.

Review

The final schedule will be reviewed with legal counsel and participating organizations before production launch, then reviewed periodically as laws and business requirements change.
